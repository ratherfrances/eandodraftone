EARLY + OFTEN

First memories of food are often the delicious treats we eat in our grandmother's kitchens. They were the type of women who made everything from scratch. Cooking was more than just food, it was a way of showing love and taking care of one another. Comfort food; Hummingbird Cake, Red Velvet Cake, Buttermilk Pound Cake. At the Emma + Ollie bakeshop you can find elevated classics you grew up loving. Our menu changes according to what is fresh and in season. We pride ourselves on using only fresh ingredients; no processed foods, no artificial sweeteners, and no funny business...just the way Emma + Ollie would have wanted it! We want all who come to Emma + Ollie to feel they have stepped into an elevated version of their own grannies' kitchen.WE BELIEVE in from scratch only. WE BELIEVE in sugar and butter over artificial sweeteners and chemicals. WE BELIEVE in using meat that's free of antibiotics. WE BELIEVE in creating less waste, and using recyclable materials like plant-based plastic. WE BELIEVE in passing down family recipes from generation to generation. WE BELIEVE in the strength of our grandmothers and of women. WE BELIEVE in supporting Texas farmers using local ingredients when possible. WE BELIEVE strong food that's fussy, isn't fun. WE BELIEVE in the power of community in FBG.
